About the Temple

A place for devotion, traditions, and spiritual harmony

The temple is a serene sanctuary where devotees gather for rituals, festivals, and daily aarti. Rich in history and architectural beauty, it offers visitors a peaceful space to connect with the divine.

Daily Aarti

Join the morning and evening prayers that fill the temple with light, song, and devotion.

Festivals

Experience vibrant celebrations with cultural performances, traditional offerings, and community gatherings.

Receive blessings and guidance from temple priests in a welcoming environment for all visitors.
